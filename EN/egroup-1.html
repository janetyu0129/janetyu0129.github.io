Design Results and Success

Success

This project is the first large-scale project in my designer's career. I have been exposed to many new things and challenges in a short period of time. In the face of unfamiliar things, I learned that don't make preset restrictions. First, find out the problems and directions, and then search for keywords. It is believed that finding solutions to problems efficiently is also an important ability for designers, and personal experience can be the nutrient for the next design.

CIS defines choosing camera as a logo as viewing the memories captured in the user's camera as a unique life story, and life is like flowing water, with twists and turns that never return, so every minute and every second is unique. Therefore, the logo combines the characteristics of the camera and running water and outlines the appearance of the camera with one stroke, symbolizing the uniqueness and conveying the brand concept.

Design Purpose

Why

Photos record everyone's unique memories, and MakePhoto is a platform for users to store and categorize photos. Wanting to highlight that the brand is a technology company and exudes a friendly and approachable feeling, so use aqua blue and streamline as the main axis of the design.

Design Process

How

From the beginning of the brand CIS, the printing of business cards, to the design of Web and APP, the abstract ideas are continuously focused and converged bit by bit, in order to faithfully bring designs that are closer to users.
