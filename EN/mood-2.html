Anonymous Chat Room Design

#visual#uiux#app

MOOD Internet Incorporation

PROJECT SUMMARY

OVERVIEW AND SUCCESS

In response to the Valentine's Day event and to enhance mutual engagement and recurring visits among MOOD users, we designed a new feature called the 'Anonymous Chat Room.' Users can initiate conversations with others by simply choosing topics of interest. Both parties remain unaware of each other's real identities, making the interaction relaxed and pressure-free. Users can chat about anything, anytime, and anywhere. For newcomers who may be unsure how to break the ice, there is a helpful feature to assist in starting conversations. The project defined a unique color scheme for the event, created a cupid character, and established the workflow for using widget UI.

PROCESS

IDEATION AND DESIGN

Throughout the process, I drew inspiration from various Valentine's Day reference examples to create a cupid character that aligns with the product's round and friendly style. The event's UI predominantly featured a pink color scheme, complemented by the product's original main visual purple hues to evoke a romantic atmosphere befitting the love season.
