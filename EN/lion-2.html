PROJECT SUMMARY

OVERVIEW AND SUCCESS

LionBobby is a subsidiary of Lion Group, focusing on high-end travel products. Because this project wants to give users a sense of luxury, I refer to many examples and try various visual styles, fonts, and adjust the spacing of components, and realize that small changes can have different feelings.

PROCESS

IDEATION AND DESIGN

Because the LionBobby is mainly a high-end travel line, the design concept is simple and clean, and incorporates the image of the Logo hot air balloon, using hot air balloons to take travelers on an unforgettable journey.
