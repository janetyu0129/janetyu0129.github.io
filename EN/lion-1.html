PROJECT SUMMARY

OVERVIEW AND SUCCESS

Due to a large amount of information scattered around, in order to make the company's staff more convenient and fast to maintain products, the platform aims to connect the front and back systems, optimize the use process and design system, and can be published directly from the backend to the frontend. I was mainly responsible for this projects during Lion. After the homepage was redesigned and the design system was updated, 500,000 new users were successfully added within three months. Due to the large number of website users, I have learned how to integrate massive information and presentation through repeated understanding and communication.
