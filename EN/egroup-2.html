PROJECT SUMMARY

OVERVIEW AND SUCCESS

During the working period, I worked with the School of Medicine of Fu Jen University in the project of industry-university cooperation, and assisted the foundation to create an official website collecting medical knowledge related to breast cancer. This project is quite special, because the client has already designed a part of the web page, and completed the remaining pages in the process to achieve design consistency, learned to empathize and strike a balance between persistence and compromise, and also participated in coding and learn how to use the Pug/Sass architecture.

PROCESS

IDEATION AND DESIGN

At first, I faced this project with a serious mood, and tried to discuss the existing page style with the client from the perspective of a design consultant. But after some understanding and familiarity, I hope that with colorful and warm feelings, users can learn about breast cancer with a positive attitude.
