PROJECT SUMMARY

OVERVIEW AND SUCCESS

Continuing the revamp of the app design style, the homepage UI has been adjusted and modularized for design consistency. In the process of redesigning the app homepage UI, not only the UI design must be considered, but also the graphic designers and engineers need to communicate, collaborate and continuously design reviewed. Only by relying on teamwork can make a better and more complete app UI, and seen the results was launched, the touching mood will make the previous efforts worthwhile.

PROCESS

IDEATION AND DESIGN

Use cards to distinguish different types of functions and services, use the gradients that appear in the key visual and large-area color blocks to match the UI and use rounded corners to present a more vivid, lively and approachable sense of life, and use big fonts for key messages in order to increase the visual focus and convey information to users more accurately, the materials should be selected with a clear subject and a clean background as much as possible.
