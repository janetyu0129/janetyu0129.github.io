Design Results and Success

Success

Because this project wants to give users a sense of luxury, I refer to many examples and try various visual styles, fonts, and adjust the spacing of components, and realize that small changes can have different feelings.

Design Purpose

Why

Because the LionBobby is mainly a high-end travel line, the design concept is simple and clean, and incorporates the image of the Logo hot air balloon, using hot air balloons to take travelers on an unforgettable journey.
