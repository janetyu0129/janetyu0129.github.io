Design Purpose

Why

Due to a large amount of information scattered around, in order to make the company's staff more convenient and fast to maintain products, the platform aims to connect the front and back systems, optimize the use process and design system, and can be published directly from the backend to the frontend.

Design Process

How

During the process, I participated in the redesign of the old homepage and imported it into RWD, redefine the design kit, and standardized and integrated all modules into the design system.

After sorting out and designing, marketing staff can freely assemble pages and put products on and off the shelves by adding new columns and modules, which greatly improves the efficiency of website maintenance.

Integrate requirements, design suitable modules, and summarize them into the design system, so that page changes can be varied and flexible, and can control consistency and facilitate future search and maintenance.

Design Results and Success

Success

I was mainly responsible for this projects during Lion. After the homepage was redesigned and the design system was updated, 500,000 new users were successfully added within three months. Due to the large number of website users, I have learned how to integrate massive information and presentation through repeated understanding and communication.
