2C&2B Website Design Integration And Build With WordPress

ASUS Cloud Corporation

The lighter color of the old 2B website is very different from the style of the 2C website, which is easy to confuse users, and often receives feedback that it is a fraudulent website.

Design Purpose

Why

In order to integrate the company's 2C and 2B websites, improve design consistency, and optimize the internal website maintenance process, it was decided to move the website to Wordpress. During the process, the information architecture and design systems of the two websites were redesigned, and the newly design concepts were updated to Wordpress.

Design Process

How

The styles of the two websites must be consistent, but they must be slightly different, so that they can be approachable to ordinary users and professional technology images for enterprises. After research and redefinition, through the difference in color, hand-drawn and solid images lead to two website styles.

Design Results and Success

Success

Integrate the company's 2C and 2B homepage into Wordpress, successfully improve the consistency of website design, reduce users' doubts, reduce the time cost of engineers and speed up the operation process on the demand side. In the process, I learned how to build a website from scratch to a complete launch and maintenance of the website.
