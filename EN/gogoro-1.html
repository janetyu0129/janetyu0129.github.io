PROJECT SUMMARY

OVERVIEW AND SUCCESS

In order to promote the launch of the new co-branded credit card with Taishin Bank, a marketing event "Swap Battle M" was designed based on the gaming concept. The gameplay is to use the behavior of daily battery swaps to divide users into teams and accumulate points and bonuses. The team with the highest points will win the Gostation® bonus! Because users need to operate with app UI, the UI is constantly adjusted and tested in terms of better user experiences presented to users. One week after the one-month event was launched, more than 10,000 new cards have been successfully added.

PROCESS

IDEATION AND DESIGN

Using the process of daily riding to Gostation® to change batteries as a starting point, the situation of sports and game competitions is integrated. The key visual style adopts dark technology and has a sense of mystery. The visuals include 2D, 3D, static and dynamic materials. The elements mainly use the texture of blurry glass and matte surface, and are matched with the color rendering of the three teams of "bouncing spring yellow", "power generation green", and "screw diver purple", emphasizing the experience of future technology, stacking the richness of visual and color.

3D elements made by Muzixiii Studio

The animations such as the dynamic increase of numbers, the movement of the game panel from bottom to top, the growth of the progress bar, and the rotation of objects are all to enhance the lively and richness of visual effects.

The dynamic presentation of the game results page uses the way of falling coins to increase the favorability of getting discounts, and clicks on other team records to use 3D rotation to make the transition effect smoother.
