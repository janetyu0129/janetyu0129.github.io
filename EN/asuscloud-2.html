Uploading List Optimization

#uiux#develope#web

ASUS Cloud Incorporation

PROJECT SUMMARY

OVERVIEW AND SUCCESS

The product optimization plan was started after many users reported that the upload list experience was not good and the data could not be uploaded as expected. The goal is to optimize the uploading function and successfully reduce 80% of customer complaints after the optimization is launched. I learned that product function optimization requires careful operation of each design step and process, and each adjustment is to present a better experience to users.

PROCESS

IDEATION AND DESIGN

After collecting users' problems, we mainly redesigned the status display of the overall progress bar, strengthened the difference between different uploading statuses and failed feedback message prompts. Discuss and analyze user problems with the project manager and customer service team during the process, and add my own ideas for optimization. Together with the prototypes, users can experience the optimization results in a more specific way, and quickly get feedback after optimization.

Before Optimization

  1. The cross-page progress bar is easy to cause the user's illusion that the waiting time is too long.
  2. The overall progress bar cannot judge the current overall status.
  3. The feedback prompt of upload failure is not obvious, and can not know the reason for the failure.

After Optimization

  1. The progress bar is shortened to make it easier to read the information, and the status of progress, success and failure is added to the overall progress bar.
  2. The overall progress bar uses a dark background to enhance the contrast and difference of colors in different states.
  3. Added a prompt message when uploading fails, explaining the reason for each file failure.
